Reason: Procrastination and lack of prioritization lead to last-minute cramming.
Recommendation: Use a planner or apps like Notion or Google Calendar to manage time effectively.

Reason: Without clear academic goals, students lack motivation to succeed.
Recommendation: Set SMART goals (Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound) and track progress.

Reason: Missing lectures leads to gaps in understanding key concepts.
Recommendation: Attend all classes, actively participate, and take proper notes.

Reason: Being surrounded by unmotivated or distracted peers can impact academic performance.
Recommendation: Choose friends who encourage good study habits and academic excellence.

Reason: Students struggle due to limited access to study materials or poor learning environments.
Recommendation: Use online learning platforms like Coursera, Khan Academy, and Google Scholar for extra learning.

Reason: Stress and fear of failure can negatively impact performance in exams.
Recommendation: Practice mindfulness, deep breathing techniques, and attempt past question papers to gain confidence.

Reason: Memorization without understanding leads to forgetting concepts quickly.
Recommendation: Focus on deep learning strategies such as concept mapping and the Feynman technique.
